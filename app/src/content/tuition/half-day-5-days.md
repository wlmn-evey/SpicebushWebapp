# Half Day - 5 Days Tuition

Our half-day five-day program offers consistent, affordable Montessori education Monday through Friday mornings.

## Tuition Rate
- **Annual Tuition:** $12,000
- **Program:** Half Day - 5 Days (Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM)
- **School Year:** 2025-2026

## Rate Structure
This is a fixed tuition rate that applies to all families regardless of income level. The rate reflects the core educational program without income-based adjustments.

## What's Included
- Complete three-hour Montessori work cycle
- Snack time
- Outdoor activities (weather permitting)
- All educational materials and supplies
- Progress assessments and conferences

## Payment Options
- Annual payment (discount may apply)
- Monthly payment plan available
- Financial aid available based on need

Extended care is not available for half-day programs.
