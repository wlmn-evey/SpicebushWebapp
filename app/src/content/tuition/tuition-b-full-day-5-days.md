# Tuition B - Full Day 5 Days

Reduced tuition rate for middle-income families seeking full-day Montessori education.

## Tuition Rate
- **Annual Tuition:** $14,348.75
- **Extended Care:** $3,600 annually (available Monday-Thursday)
- **Program:** Full Day - 5 Days (Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM)
- **School Year:** 2025-2026

## Income Qualifications
This rate applies to families with household income at or above:
- **2-person household:** $65,405
- **3-person household:** $82,400
- **4-person household:** $99,498
- **5-person household:** $113,000
- **6-person household:** $133,900
- **7-person household:** $150,380
- **8+ person household:** $167,890

## What's Included
- Complete three-hour morning work cycle
- Lunch and rest period
- Afternoon activities and outdoor time
- All educational materials and supplies
- Progress assessments and conferences

## Extended Care Option
Extended care is available Monday through Thursday from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM for an additional $3,600 annually. Friday pickup is at 3:00 PM sharp.

This rate provides significant tuition assistance while maintaining the full educational program quality.
