# Tuition C - Full Day 5 Days

Substantial tuition assistance for lower-middle income families seeking full-day care.

## Tuition Rate
- **Annual Tuition:** $11,304
- **Extended Care:** $3,400 annually (available Monday-Thursday)
- **Program:** Full Day - 5 Days (Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM)
- **School Year:** 2025-2026

## Income Qualifications
This rate applies to families with household income at or above:
- **2-person household:** $34,480
- **3-person household:** $43,440
- **4-person household:** $52,400
- **5-person household:** $61,350
- **6-person household:** $70,320
- **7-person household:** $79,280
- **8+ person household:** $88,240

## What's Included
- Complete three-hour morning work cycle
- Lunch and rest period
- Afternoon activities and outdoor time
- All educational materials and supplies
- Progress assessments and conferences

## Extended Care Option
Extended care is available Monday through Thursday from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM for an additional $3,400 annually. Friday pickup is at 3:00 PM sharp.

This rate provides significant financial assistance while maintaining the same high-quality educational experience as all our programs.
