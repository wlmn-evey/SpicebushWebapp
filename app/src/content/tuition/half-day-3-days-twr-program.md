# Half Day - 3 Days (TWR) Program

Our three-day half program provides an excellent introduction to Montessori education with maximum flexibility for families. Children attend Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday mornings.

## Schedule
- **Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday  
- **Hours:** 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM (3.5 hours daily)
- **Total Weekly Hours:** 10.5 hours

## Program Features
- Complete three-hour Montessori work cycle
- Snack time
- Outdoor activities (weather permitting)
- Mixed-age learning environment

## Ideal For
- Families seeking maximum scheduling flexibility
- Children new to Montessori education
- Budget-conscious families
- Families with stay-at-home caregivers

This program offers our most affordable option while maintaining the core Montessori educational experience. Extended care is not available for half-day programs.
