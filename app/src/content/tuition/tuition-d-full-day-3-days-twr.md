# Tuition D - Full Day 3 Days (TWR)

Maximum tuition assistance for families with the greatest financial need seeking our three-day program.

## Tuition Rate
- **Annual Tuition:** $3,244
- **Extended Care:** Included at no additional cost (available Tuesday-Thursday)
- **Program:** Full Day - 3 Days (Tuesday-Thursday, 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM)
- **School Year:** 2025-2026

## Income Qualifications
This rate applies to families with household income **less than**:
- **2-person household:** $34,480
- **3-person household:** $43,440
- **4-person household:** $52,400
- **5-person household:** $61,350
- **6-person household:** $70,320
- **7-person household:** $79,280
- **8+ person household:** $88,240

## What's Included
- Complete three-hour morning work cycle
- Lunch and rest period
- Afternoon activities and outdoor time
- All educational materials and supplies
- Progress assessments and conferences
- **Extended care at no additional charge** (Tuesday-Thursday until 5:30 PM)

## Extended Care Benefit
Families qualifying for Tuition D receive extended care at no additional cost Tuesday through Thursday from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM.

This rate makes our most flexible program option accessible to all families, regardless of financial circumstances. Our three-day option with extended care provides excellent support for working families with limited resources.
