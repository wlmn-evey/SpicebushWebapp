# Half Day - 5 Days Program

Our five-day half program provides consistent daily Montessori education with mornings dedicated to learning and afternoons available for family time. Children attend Monday through Friday mornings.

## Schedule
- **Days:** Monday through Friday
- **Hours:** 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM (3.5 hours daily)
- **Total Weekly Hours:** 17.5 hours

## Program Features
- Complete three-hour Montessori work cycle
- Snack time
- Outdoor activities (weather permitting)
- Mixed-age learning environment
- Consistent daily routine

## Ideal For
- Working families needing morning care
- Children who benefit from daily routine
- Families seeking affordable quality education
- Children preparing for full-day programs

This program provides excellent educational value with the consistency of daily attendance while keeping costs manageable. Extended care is not available for half-day programs.
