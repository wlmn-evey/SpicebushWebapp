# Full Day - 3 Days (TWR) Program

Our three-day full program offers a balanced Montessori experience with more family time while maintaining educational continuity. Children attend Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday for full days.

## Schedule
- **Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Hours:** 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM (6.5 hours daily)
- **Total Weekly Hours:** 19.5 hours

## Program Features
- Complete three-hour morning work cycle
- Lunch and rest period
- Afternoon activities and outdoor time
- Extended care available on program days until 5:30 PM

## Ideal For
- Families wanting extended family time
- Children transitioning to longer programs
- Families with flexible schedules
- Budget-conscious families seeking quality education

Extended care is available Tuesday through Thursday from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM for an additional fee. This schedule provides excellent educational continuity while allowing for extended family time on Mondays and Fridays.
