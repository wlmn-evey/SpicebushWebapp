# Full Day - 5 Days Program

Our comprehensive full-day program offers a complete Montessori educational experience five days a week. This program provides children with extended time to engage deeply with our mixed-age learning environment and complete three-hour work cycles.

## Schedule
- **Days:** Monday through Friday
- **Hours:** 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM (6.5 hours daily)
- **Total Weekly Hours:** 32.5 hours

## Program Features
- Complete three-hour morning work cycle
- Lunch and rest period
- Afternoon activities and outdoor time
- Extended care available (Monday-Thursday until 5:30 PM)

## Ideal For
- Working families needing full-day care
- Children who thrive with longer engagement periods
- Families seeking a comprehensive Montessori experience

Extended care is available Monday through Thursday from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM for an additional fee.
