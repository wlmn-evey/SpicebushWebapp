# Half Day - 3 Days (TWR) Tuition

Our most flexible program option provides quality Montessori education three mornings a week at an accessible rate.

## Tuition Rate
- **Annual Tuition:** $10,000
- **Program:** Half Day - 3 Days (Tuesday-Thursday, 8:30 AM - 12:00 PM)
- **School Year:** 2025-2026

## Rate Structure
This is a fixed tuition rate that applies to all families regardless of income level. This represents our most affordable program option while maintaining educational quality.

## What's Included
- Complete three-hour Montessori work cycle
- Snack time
- Outdoor activities (weather permitting)
- All educational materials and supplies
- Progress assessments and conferences

## Payment Options
- Annual payment (discount may apply)
- Monthly payment plan available
- Financial aid available based on need

Extended care is not available for half-day programs. This program is perfect for families seeking an introduction to Montessori education with maximum scheduling flexibility.
