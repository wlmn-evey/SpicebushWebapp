# Tuition A - Full Day 5 Days

Our full-tuition rate for families with higher household incomes who want to support our mission of accessible Montessori education.

## Tuition Rate
- **Annual Tuition:** $18,035
- **Extended Care:** $3,800 annually (available Monday-Thursday)
- **Program:** Full Day - 5 Days (Monday-Friday, 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM)
- **School Year:** 2025-2026

## Income Qualifications
This rate applies to families with household income at or above:
- **2-person household:** $84,976
- **3-person household:** $110,240
- **4-person household:** $128,750
- **5-person household:** $150,380
- **6-person household:** $172,525
- **7-person household:** $195,597
- **8+ person household:** $216,300

## What's Included
- Complete three-hour morning work cycle
- Lunch and rest period
- Afternoon activities and outdoor time
- All educational materials and supplies
- Progress assessments and conferences

## Extended Care Option
Extended care is available Monday through Thursday from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM for an additional $3,800 annually. Friday pickup is at 3:00 PM sharp.

Families paying full tuition help subsidize reduced rates for families with lower incomes, supporting our commitment to economic diversity.
