## Detailed Content Analysis

### School Identity & Mission

#### Core Demographics
- **Age Range**: 3-6 years old (preschool/primary)
- **Educational Philosophy**: Authentic Montessori methodology
- **Tagline**: "A tailored, accessible Montessori education for each child"

#### SPICES Values Framework
- **S**ocial justice
- **P**eace
- **I**nclusion
- **C**ommunity
- **E**nvironment
- **S**implicity

#### Unique Positioning
- **Accessibility Focus**: "Everyone deserves access to quality education"
- **Non-hierarchical Structure**: Small, community-oriented school
- **Individualized Approach**: Child-directed learning with teacher as facilitator
- **Mixed-age Environment**: 3-year classroom cycles

### Educational Approach

#### Montessori Methodology
- **Child-Centered Environment**: Child-sized furniture and materials
- **Concrete, Didactic Materials**: Self-correcting learning tools
- **Teacher as Scientist**: Observation-based instruction
- **Freedom of Choice**: Children select materials of interest
- **Developmental Responsiveness**: Materials introduced when appropriate

#### Classroom Structure
- **Mixed-age Groups**: Children learn from each other
- **Three-year Cycles**: Children typically stay in same classroom
- **Prepared Environment**: Carefully designed spaces
- **Individual Lesson Matching**: Customized to each child's needs

### Operational Details

#### Location & Hours
- **Address**: 827 Concord Road, Glen Mills, PA 19342
- **Phone**: (484) 202-0712
- **Email**: information@spicebushmontessori.org
- **Hours**: Mon-Thu 7:30am-5:30pm, Fri 7:30am-3:00pm

#### Financial Model
- **FIT (Family Individualized Tuition)**: Income-based tiered system
- **4 Tuition Tiers**: A ($18,035), B, C, D ($5,397)
- **Additional Support**: PA child care subsidies, transportation stipends
- **Program Options**: Full-day, half-day, 3-day schedules
- **Before/After Care**: Tiered pricing, free for Tuition D families

#### Admissions Process
- **Non-discrimination Policy**: Welcomes all races, colors, genders, ethnicities, sexual orientations
- **Tour Requirement**: Families encouraged to tour before applying
- **Multilingual Support**: Application materials in 9 languages
- **Contact-First Approach**: Direct communication encouraged
