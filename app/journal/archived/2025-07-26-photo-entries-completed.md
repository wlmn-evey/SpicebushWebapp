## 🔧 Technical Implementation Details

- **Primary Focal Point**: Most important visual element (weight 10)
- **Secondary Focal Point**: Supporting element (weight 5-8)
- **Mobile Crop Zone**: Optimized for small screens
- **Tablet Crop Zone**: Balanced view for medium screens

### SEO Optimization
- **Filename Structure**: `{category}-montessori-{subject}-{action}-{context}.webp`
- **Alt Text**: Comprehensive descriptions for accessibility
