## Welcome to Our Toddler Community

Our Toddler Program provides a warm, nurturing environment specifically designed for the developmental needs of very young children.

## Program Highlights

- **Small group size** with dedicated, trained guides
- **Child-sized environment** promoting independence
- **Language-rich activities** supporting early communication
- **Movement and sensory exploration**
- **Gentle transition** to school life

## What Your Toddler Will Experience

### Self-Care Skills
- Washing hands
- Putting on shoes and jackets
- Preparing snacks
- Toileting support

### Social Development
- Grace and courtesy lessons
- Parallel and cooperative play
- Conflict resolution skills
- Community building

### Fine and Gross Motor Development
- Pouring, scooping, and transferring activities
- Art and craft experiences
- Outdoor play and nature walks
- Music and movement

## A Typical Morning

Our consistent routine helps toddlers feel secure and confident:

- Welcome and settling in
- Free choice of activities
- Snack preparation and eating together
- Outdoor exploration
- Circle time with songs and stories
- Gentle transitions throughout
