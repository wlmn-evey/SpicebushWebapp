## About Our Primary Program

Our Primary Program provides a carefully prepared Montessori environment where children ages 3-6 can develop independence, concentration, and a love of learning.

## Key Features

- **Mixed-age classrooms** fostering peer learning and leadership
- **Hands-on Montessori materials** in all curriculum areas
- **Individualized learning** at each child's own pace
- **Outdoor education** and nature exploration
- **Peace education** and conflict resolution skills

## Daily Schedule

- 8:30-9:00: Arrival and morning work
- 9:00-11:30: Uninterrupted work cycle
- 11:30-12:00: Outdoor play
- 12:00-12:45: Lunch and cleanup
- 12:45-1:30: Quiet time/nap for younger children
- 1:30-2:45: Afternoon work or special activities
- 2:45-3:00: Closing circle
- 3:00-3:30: Dismissal

## Curriculum Areas

### Practical Life
Children develop coordination, concentration, and independence through real-world activities.

### Sensorial
Materials that refine the senses and build a foundation for mathematical thinking.

### Language
A rich language environment supporting emergent literacy through speaking, writing, and reading.

### Mathematics
Concrete materials that make abstract concepts tangible and understandable.

### Cultural Studies
Geography, science, art, and music integrated throughout the curriculum.
