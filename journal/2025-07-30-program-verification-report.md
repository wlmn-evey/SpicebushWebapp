# Spicebush Montessori Program Verification Report
Date: 2025-07-30

## Verification Summary

Based on my review of the live spicebushmontessori.org website, here are the verification results for the program information:

### 1. Program Hours: 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM
**STATUS: PARTIALLY VERIFIED**
- The website shows the core program runs from 8:30 AM with dismissal at 2:45 PM - 3:00 PM
- Actual schedule found on About page:
  - Before Care/Arrival: 7:30 am-8:30 am
  - Work Cycle: 8:30 am-11 am
  - Outdoor/Gross Motor Play: 11 am-12 pm
  - Lunch: 12 pm-12:30 pm
  - Nap or Work Cycle: 12:30 pm-2:30 pm
  - Dismissal: 2:45 pm-3 pm
  - After Care: 3 pm-5:30 pm (Monday-Thursday)

### 2. Days: Monday - Friday
**STATUS: NOT EXPLICITLY VERIFIED**

**STATUS: VERIFIED**
